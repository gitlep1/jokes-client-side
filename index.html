Walk In Jokes

A woman is sitting down in a plane, After sometime the stewardess hands out peanuts and drinks to everyone.
The woman is enjoying her drink when she hears "You're very pretty"
She looks to her left and right to thank the person but no one is sitting next to her so she plays it off.
*5 minutes later* she hears "you're eyes are gorgeous!"
Confused she looks around but no one is paying any attention to her.
So she asked the stewardess "who keeps saying those nice things about me?"
The stewardess replies "Oh, those are the peanuts, They’re complimentary."
